The daisy looks like a simple flower,but it is a actually a composite of several different parts joing to form the flower.
Although many insects visit the flower each day,the daisy is not bothered by any of them.
Generally,a daisy is white with a yellow center,although sometimes it can be pink or rose color.
Throughout the history,the daisy has been featured in myth literary works and legend.

Description

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with short creeping rhizomesand rosettes of small rounded or spoon shaped leaves that are from 3/4 to 2 inches long and grow flat to the ground.
The species habitually colonises lawns,and is difficult to eradicate by mowing-hence the term 'lawn daisy'.
It exhibits the phenomenon of heliotropism where the flowers follow the position of the sun in the sky.

The daisy looks like a simple flower,but it is a actually a composite of several different parts joing to form the flower.
Although many insects visit the flower each day,the daisy is not bothered by any of them.
Generally,a daisy is white with a yellow center,although sometimes it can be pink or rose color.
Throughout the history,the daisy has been featured in myth literary works and legend.
